Understanding Anxiety

Anxiety is a normal reaction to stress but can become overwhelming. Understanding its symptoms, causes, and treatment can help manage it effectively.

Get Help Now

What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease about something with an uncertain outcome. While occasional anxiety is normal, persistent and excessive worry can interfere with daily activities.

Common Symptoms of Anxiety

  • Feeling nervous, restless, or tense
  • Having a sense of impending danger or panic
  • Increased heart rate
  • Rapid breathing (hyperventilation)
  • Sweating and trembling
  • Difficulty concentrating or thinking about anything other than the present worry

Types of Anxiety Disorders

There are several types of anxiety disorders, including:

  • Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)
  • Panic Disorder
  • Social Anxiety Disorder
  • Specific Phobias
  • Separation Anxiety Disorder

Coping Strategies

Self-Care

Engage in regular physical activity, eat a balanced diet, and get enough sleep to manage anxiety.

Talk Therapy

Therapies like Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) can help manage anxiety symptoms effectively.

Social Support

Stay connected with supportive friends and family. Social support is crucial in managing anxiety.
